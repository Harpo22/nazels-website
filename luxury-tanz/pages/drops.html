How to Use Tanning Drops

01
Choose Your Shade

Pick Light, Medium, or Dark drops depending on your desired depth. Start light — you can always build up.

02
Mix & Blend

Add 3–8 drops to your regular moisturiser or serum. Mix well in your palm before applying to clean, exfoliated skin.

03
Glow & Repeat

Allow 4–6 hours to develop. Use nightly to maintain your perfect glow or weekly for a subtle, buildable colour.
